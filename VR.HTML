Gyroscope Head-Tracking on Your Phone

Open the VR player on your phone by going to http://10.210.219.115:5173 in Chrome. After the video loads, tap the 📱 Tap for Gyro button — then physically move your phone left, right, up and down to look around inside the 360° video. Place your phone in a VR cardboard headset for a fully immersive experience.

iPhone users: requires HTTPS — use ngrok or open on the same device as the server.
